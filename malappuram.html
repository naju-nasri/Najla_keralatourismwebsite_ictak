World’s Largest Teak Plantation

A seasonal waterfall in the Kurumbalangode Village of Nilambur Taluk in Malappuram district, Adyanpara is one of the best waterfalls in Kerala. This beautiful waterfall is situated amidst the lush jungles and is said to have waters with medicinal properties. It is located, 15 km from Nilambur, which is known for being world’s largest teak plantation. During the summer season the waterfall dries up a bit but during the monsoon, it becomes a must see place in Kerala. Several exotic avifauna and migratory birds flock this sightseeing place, and thus add to its pristine and picture perfect surrounding.
